Our Features

  1. Home
  2. Features

Extra Strong

These tanks are made from the best quality virgin & food grade glastics and have a sturdy design with thin closely spaced ribs for extra strength.

Warranty

Purex thermotech 6 layer FRP insulated water storage tanks are covered by a 15-year warranty for manufacturing defects only.

Heat & Cold Resistant

The polyurethane foam layer keeps the water protected from the hot & cold atmospheric conditions outside.

Dual Foam

To further ensure that the water remains at its original temperature, we have added a second layer of foam insulation through rotational molding technology.

Double Lid with Lock

These tanks are the only tanks in the market with 2 lids, the first one is a female threaded screw lid that seals the tank tightly thus protecting water within from dust, rainwater, mosquitoes etc. the second one is a special insulated lid placed above the first one which ensures that no heat or cold effects the water within the tank from the manhole of the tank. Also, after tightening the lid the user can lock the lid with the body of the tank, to prevent irds and animals such as pigeons, monkeys etc, from contaminating the water within.

Virgin & Food Grade Material

Beyond the Poly Urethane foam insulation, the inner tank is made from 100% Virgin, Food Grade & UV Protected LLDPE Material.

UV Stabilized

The outer layer of these tanks contain special additives that protect against UV rays, which prevent cracking, chalking and loss of other physical properties of the tanks.

Prefabricated Inlets / Outlets

For further convenience of our customers these tanks come with prefabricated holes for inlets and outlets.

Electricity Saver

In winters Purex thermotech 6 layer FRP insulated water storage tanks help in reducing the electricity consumption of geysers because the water in the tank does not become cold like the atmosphere, thus reducing the electricity consumption required for heating the water. The amount of electricity that the average household will save with the use of these tanks will make these tanks free for them within the first 5 years.

100% Hygienic, Anti-Bacterial Inner Layer

The Innermost layer i.e. The one which comes in contact with water is made from 100% Virgin, food grade LLDPE Material and also Contains special Additives, which prevents the growth of Bacteria, Algae & Fungi within the tank thus ensuring the purity of the water within.

Lifting Provision

Purex Thermotech 6 Layer FRP Insulated Water Storage Tanks weigh 3 to 4 Times more than the other tanks available in the market, we have built a provision for lifting the tanks easily to higher floors without the risk of breakage.

Foam Insulation

Purex thermotech 6 layer FRP water storage tanks are insulated with a 50mm thick layer of polyurethane foam which keeps the water inside at its original filling temperature for a long period.
